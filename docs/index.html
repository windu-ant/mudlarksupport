iOS 17+ SwiftUI ANSI Color Free

What is MUDlark?

MUDlark is a full-featured MUD (Multi-User Dungeon) client for iPhone and iPad. Connect to any MUD game server over telnet/TCP and enjoy rich ANSI/xterm color rendering, powerful automation with triggers, aliases, and timers, multiple simultaneous sessions, and much more.

Whether you're a veteran MUD player or discovering text-based RPGs for the first time, MUDlark gives you a polished mobile experience with a dark terminal-style interface designed for comfortable play.

Features

🔊

Text-to-Speech Support

Have incoming game text read aloud so you can stay immersed and play more accessibly.

🎨

Full ANSI Color

16-color, 256-color, and RGB/truecolor support. See your MUD world the way it was meant to be seen.

⚡

Triggers & Aliases

Automate gameplay with pattern-matched triggers (simple or regex), command aliases with variable expansion, and repeating timers.

📡

Direct & Proxy Connections

Connect directly via TCP, or use the optional proxy relay to keep your session alive when the app is backgrounded.

🕹️

Customizable Controls

On-screen direction pad, configurable buttons, command history, and a virtual joystick for easy navigation.

📚

Multiple Sessions

Play on multiple MUD servers at the same time and switch between sessions seamlessly.

📝

Session Logging

Auto-log your sessions to text files for later review. Export or share your adventures anytime.

🌐

Built-in World List

Comes bundled with popular MUD servers including Aardwolf, Threshold RPG, BatMUD, Medievia, and many more. Add your own custom worlds too.

🔒

Privacy First

No analytics, no tracking, no ads. Your data stays on your device. Read our privacy policy.

Getting Started

1. Download MUDlark from the App Store
2. Pick a world from the built-in list or add your own
3. Tap Connect and start exploring
4. Set up triggers, aliases, and timers to power up your play

New to MUDs? A MUD is a text-based multiplayer online game; think MMO, but powered by your imagination. You type commands like north, look, or cast fireball goblin, and the game responds with rich text descriptions. It's the original online RPG experience, and it's still thriving.
